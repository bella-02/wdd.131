Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to display and sell the creative projects I complete. People can come to this site for a variety of handmade items for sale.

Audience

My audience is primarily women between the ages of 18 and 50, who have a love for handmade goods.

Dynamic elements

Images will be able to be enlarged when they are clicked, JavaScript will be used to create a template for the layout of the listings, and the user will be able to search on the site.
